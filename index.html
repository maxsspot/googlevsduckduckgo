Google vs DuckDuckGo

An analysis






WHAT ARE GOOGLE AND DUCKDUCKGO?


Google and DuckDuckGo are both things called search engines. Google and DuckDuckGo are used to crawl the web and to find information for you based off of your search.


Today, you will learn the difference between these two search engines and which one might be better for you.


POSITIVES AND NEGATIVES OF GOOGLE


Google is one of the largest and most popular search engines on the web right now. Dissapointingly, they also collect a lot of your data.


For some people, this stands to be a huge issue. For others, it's not much of a problem.


One more poor aspect of Google is their website security is poor.


A positive aspect of Google is that their results usually match exactly what you need.


So if privacy and security is not of your concern, Google would be a great choice for being your search engine.


POSITIVES AND NEGATIVES OF DUCKDUCKGO


DuckDuckGo is a popular search engine, but it's nowhere near the level of popularity that Google is at.


One of the main ideas that DuckDuckGo prides themselves on is the privacy and security of their search engine.


DuckDuckGo gets their search results from Bing. So essentialy, you are searching Bing, but privatley and securely.


If you are interested in a more private and secure search engine, DuckDuckGo would be great for you!


THE VERDICT


For those who are ok with giving up some privacy and security for good results, Google would be the search engine for you.

For those who are ok with slightly different results but with added privacy and security, pick DuckDuckGo!
